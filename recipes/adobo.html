Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is one of the most favorite food in the Philippines.

It is also a tradition to serve a adobo for birthday celebrations etc.

Ingredients:

  • chicken
  • soy sauce
  • pepper
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • oyster sauce (optional)
  • bay leaf (for fragrance)

Steps:

  1. Use a fatty cut of meat. This kind of cut will result in a more tender and flavorful dish.
  2. Marinate the meat overnight. The longer you marinate the meat, the more intense flavors you'll unlock.
  3. Low and slow cooking method. Adobo is typically cooked over low heat for a long period of time. This allows the flavors to meld together and the meat to become tender.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Adobo is a dish that can be adjusted to suit personal preferences. If you prefer a sweeter adobo, add a bit more sugar. Throw in more chili peppers if you like it hot.
