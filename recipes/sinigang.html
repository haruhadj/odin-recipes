Sinigang na Baka Recipe

Description:

Sinigang na Baka is a classic Filipino sour soup made with tender beef, assorted vegetables, and a tangy tamarind broth. It's a comforting and flavorful dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef shank or ribs, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 medium onion, peeled and quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 eggplant (talong), sliced
  • 6-8 pieces string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 2 pieces green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 2 liters water
  • 2-3 tablespoons tamarind mix (sinigang mix)
  • Salt and fish sauce (patis) to taste

Cooking Steps:

  1. In a large pot, bring 2 liters of water to a boil.
  2. Add the beef shank or ribs and let it simmer for about 1-1.5 hours or until the meat is tender. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  3. Add the onion and tomatoes to the pot and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tamarind mix and season with salt and fish sauce to taste. Adjust the level of sourness and saltiness according to your preference.
  5. Add the radish, eggplant, and string beans. Simmer until the vegetables are tender, usually around 5-7 minutes.
  6. Finally, add the green chili peppers for some extra heat. Adjust the number of chili peppers based on your spice tolerance.
  7. Remove the pot from heat and serve your Sinigang na Baka hot.
